How It Works

Three steps to not wearing your beer.

🍺

1. Pour

Crack your beer, mixed drink, whatever. Pour it into your Pinch Cup like a civilized animal.

🤏

2. Pinch

Squeeze the soft rim together. The flexible polypropylene walls pinch shut — sealing your drink inside.

🚤

3. Party

Hit that wake. Take that pothole. Your drink stays in the cup, not on your shirt.
